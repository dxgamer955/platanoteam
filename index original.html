What Is Platano Team?

Platano Team

PlatanoTeam is an independent development studio that specializes in multi-genre, 2D pixel art games.


Founded by Eric Roquel Correa in 2015, with the intention of creating video games 100% made by Puerto Ricans. In 2018, the company officially starts to release video games to multiple platforms such as Android and HTML.
